Welcome to the Local Plant Encyclopedia

Welcome to the official website for Flora of Budhanilkantha School, a collaborative initiative to document the diversity of plants around us. Nestled at the foot of the majestic Shivapuri Hills, Budhanilkantha School boasts a rich and diverse ecosystem that serves as a living classroom for its students. In our book, we have meticulously cataloged and explained about the plants found within our school This project not only highlights the flora that thrives here but also the importance of preserving biodiversity in our surroundings. Through this website, we aim to provide an interactive platform where you can explore the wonders of our school’s greenery. From towering trees to delicate wildflowers, each plant plays its own niche in maintaining the ecological balance. Whether you’re a fellow student, a nature enthusiast, or simply curious about the plant life in our school, we invite you to dive into the natural heritage of Budhanilkantha School.

We extend our deepest gratitude to everyone who supported and guided us in bringing this project to life. This book, Flora of Budhanilkantha School, is the result of collective efforts of many individuals who have helped us directly or indirectly through out this journey. First and foremost, we would like to thank the Principal, Vice Principals, and the Chief Administrative Officer of Budhanilkantha School for fostering an environment that encourages academic exploration and creativity. We are immensely grateful to our mentor, Mr Dhruba Kumar Shrestha, for his insightful guidance throughout this journey. His expertise and dedication inspired us to approach this project with enthusiasm and precision. We also owe special thanks to Professor Dharma Raj Dangol, whose profound knowledge of botany and valuable feedback enriched the quality and depth of our research. Lastly, we would like to thank our teachers and classmates for their motivation. Your support and belief in our vision have made this endeavor possible. Thankyou everyone!
